About JAFAD-GESS

Founded in 2022, JAFAD General Engineering and Supplies Services Limited (GESS) is a Sierra Leone-based firm with 70 years of combined team experience in logistics, engineering, and construction. Our global expertise, gained across Africa, USA, Europe, and Asia, delivers local and international solutions.
